Nice to meet you!

My name is Sariah Campopiano. I have officially started my first real steps to becoming a web developer. How, you may ask, did I even find this path where I would be putting myself through learning the new world of web development? Well, I am glad you asked. It all started with a worldwide pandemic. You know the one: COVID-19. My husband and I were living in Italy celebrating the news of our first pregnancy when our world shutdown. With a lockdown, we couldn't go to the schools where we taught English. We lost many hours of work. Luckily, there were some students and classes still willing to learn English over Skype or Zoom. These students and classes were the only source of money we had. I'll save you the rest of the sob story and move right to the brighter side of things. We ended up moving back to the United States, had our baby girl in Nashville, Tennessee, and my husband started his bootcamp at Nashville Software School. In between diaper changes, feedings and taking care of all the other necessary things for our daughter, my husband and I would take walks and he would talk code. Talking the new concepts he learned in class out loud helped him better understand them. In turn, I was learning a bit about code myself. I was actually following along! He graduated 6 months later then started his first job a month after that! Our lives have completely changed for the better because my husband decided to take a chance with himself and learn something new to him as well. Now it is my turn. My husband planted the seed of coding as we walked around the block all those nights ago. I have now completed my first real introduction to web development and coding. I am ready for my next step on my web development journey. Here I come, Bootcamp!
